Algorithm Presentation Assignment

Each student is assigned one algorithmic topic from the textbook. Please prepare a short 5-minute presentation on the assigned topic, including a brief explanation of its purpose, how it works, and a simple example if applicable.

Presentations will begin on Week 11-1 (5/13) and continue across several weeks. Three students will present in each session, except for the final session, which will have four students.
